Course Details

This course is designed to help Standard 3 students build a strong foundation in Mathematics. Topics covered include addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, and basic geometry. Each lesson is broken down into simple, easy-to-follow episodes with real-life examples to make learning fun and effective.

Certification

Upon completing all lessons and passing the quizzes, students will receive an Eduvi certificate of completion. This certificate validates the student's understanding of Standard 3 Mathematics and can be shared with teachers or parents as proof of progress.

Who this course is for

This course is designed for Standard 3 students aged 8–10 who want to strengthen their Math skills. It is also helpful for parents who want to support their child's learning at home, and for teachers looking for structured lesson material.

What you'll learn in this course:

  • How to add and subtract numbers up to 1000
  • How to multiply and divide using simple methods
  • Understanding fractions and how to use them
  • Basic shapes and measurements in geometry
  • How to solve simple word problems confidently
